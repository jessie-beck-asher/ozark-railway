Where Your Donation Goes

Seven miles of century-old track take a beating from weather, weight, and one very determined locomotive. Each season the volunteer track gang replaces worn ties and spikes by hand, the same way the line was built in 1926.

The Hollow Creek & Northern Railway is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit run entirely by volunteers. Every sponsorship goes straight into the track fund — steel, timber, and the occasional pot of depot coffee for the gang that drives it all home.
